Privacy Policy for UnitSpark

Last Updated: June 2026

UnitSpark respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application, specifically aligned with standard Data Safety requirements.

1. Information We Collect (Data Safety)

To provide user authentication, account security, and tailored educational services, we collect the following data:

  • Username: Collected for account identity, personalization, and user profile management.
  • Password: Collected and processed securely for user authentication (stored using industry-standard secure cryptographic hashing).
  • Authentication Identifiers: Tokens or account credentials used to verify your identity upon login and maintain a secure session.
  • Device Identifier (Device ID): Collected to secure your account and enforce account security rules, such as preventing unauthorized multi-device access.
  • Academic/Verification Details: Collected to verify user registration status and provide appropriate access to curated academic content, courses, and chapters.
  • App Progress & Quiz Data: We store your quiz results, study progress, and performance metrics to generate smart recommendations and track your learning journey.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following specific purposes:

  • To securely authenticate your identity and grant access to your saved profile.
  • To prevent unauthorized access and protect account integrity by linking sessions to your device.
  • To deliver, personalize, and adapt educational content, quizzes, and recommendations based on your performance.
  • To respond to your customer support requests, feedback, or troubleshooting needs.

3. Data Retention

  • Personal Information: Usernames, passwords, authentication data, and progress metrics are retained for as long as your account remains active to provide uninterrupted service.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous or aggregated usage data may be temporarily retained to optimize app performance and improve learning content.
  • Transaction/Access Records: System and verification logs may be retained for up to 1 year for security compliance and accounting purposes.

4. Account Deletion Rights

You have the right to request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

  • To request account deletion, please send an email to naolt3100@gmail.com from your registered email address, specifying your username.
  • Upon receiving your request, we will process it and completely delete your personal information—including your username, login credentials, authentication tokens, performance data, and device linking records—within 5 business days.

5. Security of Your Data

We implement strict technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Your credentials and passwords are encrypted and never stored or transmitted in plain text.
